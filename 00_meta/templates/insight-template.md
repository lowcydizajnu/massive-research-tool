# Insight — {Topic}

An insight is a finding distilled from literature, interviews, or both — packaged so that designers and architects can act on it. Every insight must cite its evidence and state what it implies for the product.

- **Status:** draft | in review | validated | superseded
- **Evidence basis:** {e.g. literature synthesis (N papers), 3 interviews, mixed}
- **Confidence:** low | medium | high
- **Source materials:** {Consensus search URLs, interview note files, etc.}
- **Last updated:** YYYY-MM-DD

## Headline

One paragraph stating the insight in plain language. If a stakeholder reads only this, they should still come away with the right takeaway.

## Evidence

The findings behind the insight, with inline citations. Both supporting and contradicting evidence — be honest about disagreement in the literature.

## What this implies for the product

Concrete implications: which JTBDs gain priority, which ADRs are affected, which design decisions this should pressure-test.

## What this insight does NOT tell us

The limits of the evidence. What questions remain open. What would lift confidence from medium to high.

## Sources

Full bibliography with links. Group by relevance (core / supporting / peripheral) if there are more than ~8 entries.
