A big week — the highlight is one-click **study materials upload to OSF**, plus new discoverability, workspace, and gating features across the app.

## New features

### Upload study materials to OSF
Push your full study to the Open Science Framework — stimuli files, machine-readable JSON, and a protocol PDF — from the Study Record. A dedicated **Materials on OSF** panel now also appears on the **Preregister** tab so you can freeze materials at the same moment you freeze your hypotheses. See [OSF integration](/integrations/osf).

### AI persuasion chat starter
A new starter template lets participants chat with Claude, powered by your own [Anthropic API key](/integrations/anthropic). Clone it from the [Explore](https://myresearchlab.app/explore) page and adapt to your paradigm.

### Meet Researchers directory
Discover other researchers on the platform and see who is following whom. Your public [researcher profile](/workspace/researcher-profiles) now has a personal tab, and the Explore page shows richer "Researchers to follow" tiles with study and follower stats.

### Workspaces tab in Settings
Manage all your workspaces from one place: list, rename, create, and restore. Workspace rename is now surfaced directly on the Workspace settings page.

### Archive and restore a workspace
Archiving a workspace is now reversible — no destructive delete. Bring an archived workspace back at any time from the Workspaces tab.

### Home page: Start-here checklist and replication widgets
A new **Start here** pinned card walks first-time users through the getting-started steps, tracking progress on your newest study. Two new widgets — **Studies you replicated** and **Replications of yours** — surface [replication](/methodology/replication) activity at a glance.

### Global Activity and What's-New bells
Two new bells in the top nav: **Activity** (grouped, per-item read state) and **What's New**. Notifications now fan out inline, so your Activity feed stays responsive.

### Social-post interaction gating
Require participants to react, comment, or Report before they can continue. Configure requirements per [block](/builder/blocks), see live chips in-runtime, and toggle a compact interaction summary. Report is opt-in and fully recorded.

### Branch on a social-post reaction
Route participants down different paths based on the reaction they picked, from the [Conditions](/builder/conditions) panel — no free-text matching, just a dropdown.

### In-screen conditional reveal
Reveal follow-up content on the same screen instead of navigating to a new one. Great for tight follow-up questions and dependent stimuli.

### Duplicate and delete from tiles
Study tiles now have a ⋯ menu with duplicate and delete. Block tiles inside a study support the same, and you can duplicate a block directly from the Configure panel.

## Updates

- **Product renamed to My Research Lab.** New name, same product; the app footer and legal pages have been updated to match.
- **Faster navigation.** Instant loading skeletons when switching sections — no more blank screens.
- **EU compute region.** App compute is pinned to Frankfurt (Vercel `fra1`) — details on the [data and security](/trust/data-and-security) page.
- **Explore leads with starter covers.** Real cover images for starter templates, and the use-case band has been dropped.
- **Misinformation starter** now renders posts Facebook-style out of the box.
- **Social-post branding** simplified — one logo toggle replaces the earlier branding tiers.
- **Comment counts** bump when a participant leaves a comment.
- **Social-post feed** un-boxes posts, with a gate top-bar and a fake-nav toggle for tighter simulations.

## Bug fixes

- **OSF image uploads** now resolve correctly when the source is a media-gateway URL.
- **Researchers directory** no longer crashes on large result sets.
- **Builder** — the condition allocation badge wraps to fit the left panel.
- **Study runtime** — question blocks are properly boxed on the social-feed background, and light studies now get light native controls (via `color-scheme` from the theme).
- **Onboarding** — fixed a redirect leak, scoped the Start-here checklist to the current workspace, excluded Preview sessions from the "opened recruitment" step, and reworked the checklist into a pinned card.
- **Data export** — social-post replies (with parent comment) and comment likes are now recorded.
- **Social-post** — static post-copy size, one consistent Like size, honest comment count, inline reply that also works in preview, and unrevealed conditional blocks no longer block Continue.
- **Versions** — restoring a saved version now brings back the full snapshot and its conditions, so drift clears cleanly.
- **Conditions** — stray empty strings are stripped from reaction conditions.
