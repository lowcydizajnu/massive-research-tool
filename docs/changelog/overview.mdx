<Update label="July 6, 2026" description="Weekly release">

We renamed the product to **My Research Lab** and shipped a big week for OSF, onboarding, and workspace management.

## New features

- **Upload study materials to OSF.** From the Preregister tab, push your study's stimuli, blocks, and a protocol PDF straight into your OSF project — no manual export. See [OSF integration](/integrations/osf).
- **AI persuasion chat starter.** A new starter template lets participants chat with Claude inside a study. Bring your own Anthropic key. See [Anthropic integration](/integrations/anthropic).
- **Meet Researchers directory.** Discover other researchers on the platform and view their public [profiles](/workspace/researcher-profiles).
- **Workspaces tab in Settings.** List, rename, create, and restore your workspaces from one place. See the [study workspace](/builder/study-workspace) guide.
- **Archive & restore workspaces.** Archiving is fully reversible — no destructive deletes.
- **Getting-started checklist.** A pinned Start-here card on the home page walks new accounts through their first study, and a "Take the guided tutorial" button is always one click away. See the [quickstart](/getting-started/quickstart).
- **Duplicate & delete from tile menus.** Studies and blocks now have a ⋯ menu to duplicate or delete in place. You can also duplicate a block directly from the Configure panel. See [Blocks](/builder/blocks).

## Updates

- **My Research Lab.** The product has been renamed from Massive Research Lab, with refreshed legal pages and app footer.
- **EU compute region.** All app compute is now pinned to Frankfurt (fra1) for lower latency and clearer data residency. See [Data and security](/trust/data-and-security).
- **Faster section switches.** Instant loading skeletons make navigating between sections feel snappier.
- **Richer Explore page.** Starter templates now lead with real cover images, and researcher tiles show follower and study stats. The misinformation starter renders posts Facebook-style.
- **Interaction summary controls.** Toggle the summary on or off, with compact emoji chips and translatable labels.
- **Simpler social-post branding.** Branding tiers collapsed into a single logo toggle.

## Bug fixes

- Fixed a crash on the /researchers page when the follow limit exceeded the max.
- Fixed the Builder condition-allocation badge so it wraps to fit the left panel.
- Social-feed studies now box each question block cleanly, and light themes get light native controls on mobile.
- Exports now include social-post replies (with their parent comment) and comment likes.
- Onboarding fixes: the Start-here checklist is workspace-scoped, Preview sessions no longer count toward the "opened recruitment" step, and a redirect leak on the guided tour is resolved.
- Restored the working reaction picker on social posts, fixed a conditional block that could block Continue, and made the "Just the post" tier honor the social design.

</Update>
