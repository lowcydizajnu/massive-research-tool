## The five tabs

### Connections
Connect recruitment providers so you can launch studies on their participant pools. Each provider is authenticated once per workspace — all studies in the workspace draw from the same connection.

Currently live: **Prolific** (global participant pool, pay-per-response). Coming soon: **CloudResearch** (US-focused) and **Sona Systems** (credit-based recruitment for university psychology departments).

To connect a provider, paste your Personal Access Token (PAT) from your provider account. The app stores the token securely and uses it to access your account and push study details on-demand.

**Live updates** — once connected, you can enable webhooks so Prolific notifies the app the moment submissions arrive or study status changes. When on, participant counts and recruitment progress update without a page refresh. When off, the app polls Prolific every ~10 minutes and reconciles state on page load.

### Open recruitment
A read-only provider-side view of every study currently recruiting on Prolific in your workspace. For each study, you see:
- Submission counts broken down by status: **Started**, **Awaiting review**, **Approved**, **Rejected**, **Timed out**
- The reward amount and allocation (how many spots are filled)
- Links to manage the study on Prolific or jump to the [Run stage](/builder/study-workspace) in the app

This mirrors recruitment health and response flow from the provider's perspective. (For your own-side recruitment health — drop-off, time-to-completion — see the [Run stage](/builder/study-workspace).)

### Panels
Panels are **workspace-scoped cohorts** of past participants, identified by their anonymous provider ID. Use panels to re-recruit known participants in a new study or exclude them to prevent cross-contamination.

Create a new panel by name and description, then add members by bulk-importing submissions from a study you've already run. When adding members, you can filter by completion status (**Completed** — approved + awaiting review; **Approved only**; **Everyone who started**).

Members are always displayed by opaque ID — the app never exposes personally identifiable information. Each member's record notes which study they first appeared in.

Delete a panel to remove its membership tracking; the participants themselves are unaffected.

### Compensation
A **read-only mirror** of participant spend synced from your provider (Prolific charges you directly, not through the app). All figures are grouped by currency — no FX blending.

**Spend overview:**
- Total spend (all-time, last 30 days) broken down by currency
- Count of participants paid
- Average cost per participant

**By study** — a table showing reward, bonus, and total cost per study.

**Last 6 months** — a bar chart of spend over time.

**Recent payouts** — the latest 50 transactions, each recording when the payment was made, which study it belongs to, whether it was a reward or bonus, the amount, and who approved it (manually by a team member, or automatically on provider).

**Monthly budget (owner/admin only):** Set an optional advisory budget limit and alert threshold. When this month's spending crosses the threshold, the app flags a warning at the top of the page. The budget is advisory only — nothing is blocked, and your provider charges you regardless.

**Auto-approval (owner/admin only):** When enabled, submissions with no open quality flags are automatically approved once they've been awaiting review for a set number of hours (default 24). Flagged or manually rejected submissions always stay manual. This keeps the approval queue moving without sacrificing oversight.

### Quality
Cross-study submission quality review and response management. Flag patterns — fast completion, straight-lining, duplicate participants, failed attention checks, possible spam — are surfaced in a centralized queue alongside inline response previews so you can make informed decisions.

**Flags and resolution:**
- Each flag shows its **kind** (e.g., "Straight-lining"), **severity** (high/medium/low), the study it came from, the participant's opaque ID, and any detail
- Expand any flag to view the participant's response — all item answers are shown so you can spot the problem yourself
- Resolve a flag by approving (marks them complete on the provider — they are paid), rejecting (marks incomplete on the provider — they are not paid, and Prolific notifies them with your reason), sending a bonus, or dismissing (records your decision, no payment action)

**Bulk actions:** Select multiple flags at once and resolve them all together (approve, reject, or dismiss).

**Re-scan:** Manually trigger a quality scan of recent submissions to find new flags. (The app also scans automatically as studies collect responses.)

**Two tabs:**
- **Needs review** — open flags awaiting your decision
- **Resolved** — flags you've already approved, rejected, dismissed, or bonused

<Tip>
All money operations (approve, reject, bonus) are one-click on Prolific — the app asks for confirmation before executing, but your provider handles the actual payment. Never manually approve or reject a submission on the provider's side while it's flagged here; use the Quality tab to stay in sync.
</Tip>

## Role-based access

- **Owners and admins** can connect/disconnect providers, set budgets, enable auto-approval, and resolve quality flags
- **Members** can view all Participants tabs but cannot make changes
- **Viewers** can see Participants but have no edit access
